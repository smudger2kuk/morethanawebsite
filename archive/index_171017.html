WEB DESIGN

We build all sites for mobile first - since late 2016 more mobile devices than desktops are viewing web content.

This approach has advantages over just catering for more visitors to your site; Google and other search engines now consider a good mobile experience as a positive factor when ranking your website in their search results.

All of our sites are designed and developed from the ground up, meaning we fully control how your site will look across all devices.

We don't use templates or frameworks in our development, in fact we take pride in that everything we deliver is coded in-house with no reliance on bloated frameworks.

We fine-tune all text, links, images and other media right from the outset to make sure your site is ready for the Google bots.

We leave no stone unturned, including setting up your Google analytics and registering your site with the search engines.

You can rest assured everything will be 100% ready to give your website the best possible start in the quest for first page status!
